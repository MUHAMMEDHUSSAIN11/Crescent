About Us

Welcome to Crescent Public School, a premier educational institution established in 1998 and affiliated to CBSE Delhi. Our school is located in Mannancherry, Alappuzha, Kerala and offers classes from Kindergarten to 10th grade. We take pride in providing quality education to our students in a positive and inclusive learning environment. In addition to academic excellence, we also encourage our students to participate in extracurricular activities. Our school boasts of extensive sports facilities including a football ground, basketball court, and cricket ground. At Crescent Public School, we prioritize academic excellence while fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment.
