About Us

Improving lives through Learning.

Welcome to Crescent Public School, a premier educational institution established in 1998 and affiliated to CBSE Delhi. Our school is located in Mannancherry, Alappuzha, Kerala and offers classes from Kindergarten to 10th grade. We take pride in providing quality education to our students in a positive and inclusive learning environment. In addition to academic excellence, we also encourage our students to participate in extracurricular activities. Our school boasts of extensive sports facilities including a football ground, basketball court, and cricket ground. At Crescent Public School, we prioritize academic excellence while fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing personalized attention to each student, ensuring their overall growth and success. With a strong emphasis on character building and values, we strive to instill qualities of discipline, integrity, and compassion in our students. Crescent Public School is not just an educational institution; it's a community where students are encouraged to explore their potential, embrace challenges, and become responsible global citizens. Come, be a part of our community and embark on a journey of discovery and growth with Crescent Public School.
